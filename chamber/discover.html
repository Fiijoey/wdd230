Statistical data

Population: As of the 2021 census, the population of Greater Accra was approximately 5,455,692.

Economy: Accra is the economic hub of Ghana, with a diverse economy that includes manufacturing, services, and trade.

Education: The region is home to several educational institutions, including the University of Ghana, one of the largest and oldest universities in the country.

Healthcare: Accra has numerous healthcare facilities, including the Korle-Bu Teaching Hospital, one of the largest teaching hospitals in West Africa.

Transportation: The city has a well-developed transportation network, including Kotoka International Airport, which serves as the main gateway to Ghana.
